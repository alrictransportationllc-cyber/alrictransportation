2. Service Description

Alric Transportation provides specialized medical supply and medication transportation services including but not limited to:

  • Prescription medication delivery
  • Medical supply transport
  • Emergency medical delivery services
  • Cold chain pharmaceutical transport
  • Laboratory specimen transport

3. Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate pickup and delivery information
  • Ensuring proper packaging of medical items
  • Compliance with all applicable regulations
  • Payment of fees according to agreed terms
  • Proper authorization for controlled substances

Important Notice

All medical transportation services are subject to state and federal regulations. Clients must ensure all items are properly documented and authorized for transport.

4. Liability and Insurance

Alric Transportation maintains comprehensive insurance coverage and follows industry best practices for secure transport. Our liability is limited to the terms specified in individual service agreements.

5. Pricing and Payment

Service fees are determined based on distance, urgency, special handling requirements, and other factors. Payment terms are net 30 days unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Cancellation Policy

Service cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before scheduled pickup time to avoid charges. Emergency services may have different cancellation terms.

HIPAA Compliance

1. Our Commitment to HIPAA

Alric Transportation is committed to protecting health information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and related regulations.

Business Associate Agreement

We execute Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with covered entities to ensure proper handling of Protected Health Information (PHI).

2. PHI Protection Measures

Physical Safeguards

Secure vehicles, locked storage containers, and restricted access to transportation areas.

Administrative Safeguards

Employee training, access controls, and incident response procedures.

Technical Safeguards

Encrypted communications, secure tracking systems, and digital access controls.

3. Employee Training

All employees receive comprehensive HIPAA training including:

  • PHI identification and handling
  • Privacy and security requirements
  • Incident reporting procedures
  • Minimum necessary standards

4. Incident Response

We maintain detailed incident response procedures for potential PHI breaches and report incidents as required by law within 24 hours of discovery.
