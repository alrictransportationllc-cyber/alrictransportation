Terms of Service

1. Agreement to Terms

By using Alric Transportation services, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our services.

2. Service Description

Alric Transportation provides medical transportation services including:

  • Medical transportation to healthcare facilities
  • Wheelchair accessible transportation
  • Stretcher transportation services
  • On-demand rides for medical and general purposes
  • Airport transportation services
  • Student transportation
  • Medication delivery services

3. Booking and Scheduling

Transportation services must be booked in advance when possible. For emergency services, we will respond as quickly and safely as possible. Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.

4. Payment Terms

Payment is due upon completion of service unless other arrangements have been made. We accept cash, credit cards, and insurance payments where applicable. Rates are determined based on distance, time, and specific service requirements.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate pickup and destination addresses
  • Be ready at the scheduled pickup time
  • Inform us of any special medical or mobility needs
  • Treat our staff with respect and courtesy
  • Follow safety instructions and vehicle policies

6. Service Limitations

We reserve the right to refuse service in cases where safety cannot be ensured, or where our equipment is not suitable for the client's needs. We are not a medical emergency service and recommend calling 911 for life-threatening emergencies.

If you experience any issues with our service, please contact us immediately at (555) 123-4567 or info@alrictransportation.com

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide safe and effective transportation services:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, phone number, email
  • Medical Information: Mobility needs, medical equipment requirements
  • Emergency Contacts: For safety and communication purposes
  • Trip Information: Pickup/drop-off locations, appointment details
  • Payment Information: Billing details and insurance information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide transportation services safely and effectively
  • Coordinate with healthcare providers when necessary
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Communicate about service updates or scheduling
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and customer experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary for emergency medical situations
  • With healthcare providers for coordination of care
  • With insurance companies for claims processing
  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage, secure transmission protocols, and limited access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of non-essential communications. Contact us to exercise these rights.

🔒 Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal and medical information in compliance with all applicable privacy laws.

HIPAA Compliance

1. Protected Health Information (PHI)

As a medical transportation provider, we may have access to your Protected Health Information. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of all PHI in accordance with HIPAA regulations.

2. Uses and Disclosures

We may use or disclose your PHI for:

  • Treatment: Coordinating with your healthcare providers
  • Payment: Processing insurance claims and billing
  • Operations: Quality improvement and staff training
  • Required by Law: When mandated by federal or state law
  • Emergency Situations: To protect your health and safety

3. Your HIPAA Rights

  • Right to access your PHI
  • Right to request amendments to your PHI
  • Right to receive an accounting of disclosures
  • Right to request restrictions on use or disclosure
  • Right to request confidential communications
  • Right to file a complaint

4. Security Measures

We maintain administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect PHI:

  • Staff training on HIPAA compliance
  • Secure storage and transmission of information
  • Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments and updates

📋 HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

Upon request, we will provide you with a complete Notice of Privacy Practices that details how we use and protect your health information.

Liability & Insurance

1. Insurance Coverage

Alric Transportation maintains comprehensive insurance coverage including:

  • Commercial vehicle liability insurance
  • Professional liability coverage
  • General liability insurance
  • Workers' compensation insurance
  • Medical payments coverage

2. Service Limitations

While we strive to provide excellent service, please understand:

  • We are not a licensed medical facility or emergency service
  • Our drivers are not medical professionals unless specifically noted
  • We cannot provide medical treatment or medical advice
  • Traffic conditions and other factors may affect arrival times

3. Client Responsibility

Clients are responsible for:

  • Accurately reporting their medical and mobility needs
  • Following safety instructions and vehicle policies
  • Bringing necessary medical equipment and medications
  • Informing us of any changes in their condition

4. Limitation of Liability

Our liability is limited to the scope of our transportation services. We are not responsible for missed appointments due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to traffic, weather, or vehicle mechanical issues.

5. Emergency Procedures

In case of medical emergency during transport:

  • We will immediately call 911 if necessary
  • We will proceed to the nearest appropriate medical facility
  • We will contact your emergency contacts
  • We will cooperate fully with emergency responders

🛡️ Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. All vehicles are regularly inspected and maintained, and our drivers undergo background checks and safety training.
