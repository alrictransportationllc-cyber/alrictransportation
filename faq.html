❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our medical transportation services

📅 Booking & Scheduling

How far in advance should I book my transportation? ▼

For routine medical appointments, we recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability. For recurring appointments (like dialysis), we can set up regular scheduling weeks or months ahead.

For emergency or urgent needs, we offer same-day service when possible. Call us immediately at (555) 123-4567 for urgent requests.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? ▼

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment. Please give us at least 2 hours notice to avoid cancellation fees. For urgent changes, call us immediately.

You can reschedule by calling (555) 123-4567 or emailing info@alrictransportation.com.

Do you provide service on weekends and holidays? ▼

Yes! We provide transportation services 7 days a week, including most holidays. Our emergency services are available 24/7.

Regular scheduled services are available Monday through Sunday. Holiday scheduling may have limited availability, so please book early.

What if my appointment runs late? ▼

We understand that medical appointments can run late. Please call or text us as soon as you know you'll be delayed. We'll do our best to accommodate delays, though extended waits may incur additional charges.

For scheduled return trips, we typically allow up to 30 minutes of wait time at no extra charge.

🚐 Our Services

What types of medical transportation do you provide? ▼

We offer comprehensive medical transportation services including:

  • Transportation to medical appointments, hospitals, and clinics
  • Wheelchair accessible vehicle transportation
  • Stretcher transportation for bed-bound patients
  • Dialysis transportation
  • Discharge transportation from hospitals
  • Transportation to rehabilitation facilities
Do you accommodate wheelchairs and mobility devices? ▼

Absolutely! Our fleet includes vehicles specifically equipped for wheelchair accessibility with:

  • Hydraulic wheelchair lifts
  • Secure wheelchair restraint systems
  • ADA-compliant accessibility features
  • Space for mobility devices and medical equipment

Please inform us about your specific mobility needs when booking so we can dispatch the appropriate vehicle.

Can family members or caregivers ride along? ▼

Yes, we welcome family members and caregivers to accompany patients. Our vehicles have additional seating available for companions at no extra charge.

Please let us know when booking if someone will be accompanying you so we can ensure adequate space in the vehicle.

Do you provide medication delivery services? ▼

Yes, we offer secure medication delivery services including:

  • Prescription medication pickup and delivery
  • Temperature-controlled transport when required
  • Secure chain of custody protocols
  • Same-day delivery for urgent medications
  • Delivery confirmation and signatures

💰 Pricing & Payment

How much does transportation cost? ▼

Our rates depend on several factors including:

  • Distance traveled
  • Type of service required (wheelchair, stretcher, etc.)
  • Time of day and day of week
  • Wait times at appointments

Contact us at (555) 123-4567 for a free quote based on your specific needs. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you accept insurance? ▼

Yes, we work with many insurance providers including Medicaid, Medicare, and private insurance plans. Coverage varies by plan and service type.

We'll verify your insurance benefits before service and help with prior authorization if required. Please bring your insurance card with you.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Cash
  • Credit and debit cards
  • Insurance (when covered)
  • Check (with prior arrangement)
  • Payment plans for ongoing services
Are there additional fees I should know about? ▼

We believe in transparent pricing. Additional fees may apply for:

  • Extended wait times beyond 30 minutes
  • Late cancellations (less than 2 hours notice)
  • Additional stops during the trip
  • Special equipment or assistance requirements

All potential fees are discussed during booking so there are no surprises.

🛡️ Safety & Policies

Are your drivers trained and licensed? ▼

Yes, all our drivers are:

  • Professionally licensed with clean driving records
  • Background checked and drug tested
  • Trained in medical transportation protocols
  • CPR and first aid certified
  • Experienced in patient care and sensitivity

We maintain high standards for all our staff to ensure your safety and comfort.

What safety measures do you have in place? ▼

Your safety is our top priority. Our safety measures include:

  • Regular vehicle maintenance and safety inspections
  • GPS tracking and communication systems
  • Emergency medical equipment in all vehicles
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage
  • 24/7 dispatch monitoring
  • COVID-19 safety protocols and vehicle sanitization
What happens in case of a medical emergency during transport? ▼

Our drivers are trained to handle medical emergencies:

  • Immediate contact with 911 emergency services
  • Basic first aid and CPR if needed
  • Direct transport to the nearest appropriate medical facility
  • Communication with your emergency contacts
  • Cooperation with emergency medical personnel

All vehicles are equipped with emergency medical supplies and communication equipment.

How do you protect my medical information? ▼

We are fully HIPAA compliant and take your privacy seriously:

  • All staff undergo HIPAA training
  • Secure storage and transmission of medical information
  • Information shared only on a need-to-know basis
  • Patient consent required for information sharing
  • Regular privacy and security audits

Your medical information is protected according to all federal and state privacy laws.
