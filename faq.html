General Questions

What is Alric Transportation and what do you do?

Alric Transportation is a specialized medical transportation company that provides secure, reliable delivery of medications, medical supplies, and healthcare-related items. We serve pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, and patients throughout the region with professional logistics solutions designed specifically for the healthcare industry.

What areas do you service?

We provide transportation services throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding regions within a 200-mile radius of our main facility. Our service area includes:

  • Urban and suburban areas
  • Rural communities
  • Hospital networks
  • Pharmacy chains and independent pharmacies

Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Are you available 24/7?

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency medical transportation services for critical deliveries. Our regular business hours are Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM, but emergency services are available around the clock, including weekends and holidays.

How do I schedule a delivery?

You can schedule a delivery through several methods:

  • Call our main number: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Email us at: info@alrictransportation.com
  • Use our online contact form on our website
  • For emergencies: +1 (555) 987-6543

We recommend scheduling at least 2 hours in advance for regular deliveries.

Services & Delivery

What types of medical items can you transport?

We can transport a wide variety of medical items including:

  • Prescription medications (including controlled substances)
  • Over-the-counter medications
  • Medical supplies and equipment
  • Laboratory specimens
  • Vaccines and biologics (temperature-controlled)
  • Medical devices
  • Hospital supplies

All items must be properly packaged and documented according to regulatory requirements.

Do you provide temperature-controlled transport?

Yes! We specialize in temperature-controlled transport for medications and medical supplies that require specific storage conditions. Our vehicles are equipped with:

  • Refrigerated compartments (2-8¬∞C)
  • Frozen storage capability (-20¬∞C)
  • Room temperature controlled environments
  • Continuous temperature monitoring
  • Data logging and reporting
How fast can you deliver in an emergency?

Emergency deliveries are our top priority. We can typically respond within:

  • 30 minutes for critical emergencies
  • 1 hour for urgent deliveries
  • 2-4 hours for same-day service

Response times may vary based on location, traffic conditions, and item preparation requirements.

Can I track my shipment?

Absolutely! We provide real-time tracking for all shipments. You can track your delivery through:

  • Our online tracking system on our website
  • SMS notifications with status updates
  • Email notifications
  • Phone updates upon request

You'll receive a tracking number when your shipment is dispatched.

Pricing & Billing

How is pricing calculated?

Our pricing is based on several factors:

  • Distance between pickup and delivery locations
  • Urgency level (emergency, same-day, scheduled)
  • Special handling requirements (temperature control, hazardous materials)
  • Time of day and day of week
  • Size and weight of items
  • Insurance value of items being transported

Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Check payments
  • Bank transfers
  • Insurance billing (when applicable)
  • Corporate accounts with net payment terms

Payment terms are typically net 30 days for established business customers.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive pricing for high-volume customers and regular service agreements. Volume discounts are available for:

  • Monthly service contracts
  • High-frequency deliveries
  • Large healthcare organizations
  • Pharmacy chains

Contact our sales team to discuss custom pricing options.

Compliance & Safety

Are you HIPAA compliant?

Yes, we are fully HIPAA compliant. We maintain strict protocols to protect patient health information (PHI) including:

  • Signed Business Associate Agreements with covered entities
  • Employee HIPAA training and certification
  • Secure handling and transport of PHI
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular compliance audits
Do you transport controlled substances?

Yes, we are licensed and authorized to transport controlled substances. We maintain:

  • DEA registration and licensing
  • Secure chain of custody procedures
  • Background-checked and trained drivers
  • Advanced security measures
  • Detailed documentation and reporting

All controlled substance transports require proper authorization and documentation.

What insurance coverage do you maintain?

We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including:

  • General liability insurance
  • Professional liability coverage
  • Commercial auto insurance
  • Cargo insurance for transported items
  • Cyber liability insurance

Insurance certificates can be provided upon request.

How do you ensure medication safety during transport?

Medication safety is our top priority. We ensure safety through:

  • Temperature-controlled vehicles with monitoring
  • Secure packaging requirements
  • Chain of custody documentation
  • Trained and certified drivers
  • GPS tracking and monitoring
  • Quality assurance procedures
  • Regular vehicle maintenance and inspections
